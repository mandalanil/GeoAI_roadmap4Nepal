Transforming Nepal's Future with GeoAI

Leveraging geospatial artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems, fostering economic growth, and empowering communities from the grassroots to the national level. This is a plan to build a resilient, efficient, and prosperous Nepal.

Short & Long-Term Goals

Nepal's Digital Landscape

The foundation for this transformation is Nepal's rapidly expanding digital infrastructure. The country has seen significant growth in both internet and mobile phone penetration over recent years, a critical factor for successful digital initiatives. The data for this chart is based on reports from the International Telecommunication Union (ITU, 2024).

The "Invest to Reinvest" Model

Digitizing elections is the first step. By reducing costs, we can create a sustainable fund to fuel Nepal's long-term digital transformation. This chart illustrates how savings from a single election cycle can be strategically reinvested. The cost data is based on estimates from the Ministry of Finance (MoF, 2023).

Savings Allocation

Based on a 40% cost reduction in election administration, funds are reallocated to sectors with the highest potential for impact.
